Fun Activities for Your Physical and Mental Health:

Active Game Night

For your next family game night, try playing some games that get you to move around a bit! Try playing games like Twister or even video games like Just Dance or an active VR game!

Coloring

After a long day at work or school, try breaking out your colored pencils, markers, or crayons and do some coloring! Coloring helps relax the amygdala, the fear center of your brain, so it allows you to relax after a stressful day.
